About me

I am a Data driven professional with 4 years of professional experience. I am fascinated by data and the possibilities in turning insights into actions. Due to my strong interest in this field, I decided to pursue a career in Data Analytics, which can be supported by my decision to leave my career behind and complete a Master's degree in Data Science. My studies equipped me with technical skills in Python, SQL and R, as well as with experience using and deploying Machine and Deep Learning models.

Having lived and worked in 4 different countries so far, I had the opportunity to meet people from various cultures and as a result this gave me a broader mindset. My previous experience was with teams which specialized in Credit, Risk and Fraud. Moreover, it allowed me to develop my organisational, presentation, liaising as well as project management skills, and also provided me with a unique view of how businesses operate.

Currently, I am looking to join a team in Data Analytics in a forward thinking, data-driven organisation. At the same time, I am also working on projects to further develop my skills and knowledge in Data Analytics. If you want to get in touch, feel free to either connect with me on LinkedIn or send me an email.
